ADA member dentists are among the most financially capable and professionally focused individuals in the country — and largely disengaged from traditional financial acquisition channels.

Our reach can scale from a focused Midwest core to full national deployment, with each tier building on the last.

This audience requires a different, highly specific approach.

Auto loans act as a consistent, always-on entry point. They capture existing demand and drive steady conversion — making them the anchor product for any BCU growth strategy.

Recreational financing signals higher-income, high-intent segments. Especially in the Lakes regions. These products benefit from strong upper-funnel presence and visual channels that connect with lifestyle and aspiration.

CDs require longer-term trust and education. They perform best with sustained engagement and nurturing — a product that rewards patience and consistent brand presence. This is a product often used by the older demographic to grow their money safely.

The strategy must balance precision with sustained presence across the full funnel.
